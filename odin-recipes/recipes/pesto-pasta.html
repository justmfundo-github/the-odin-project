Pesto Pasta

A delicious pesto pasta recipe. Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold!

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Fettucine pasta
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan

Steps/Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil
  3. Stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  4. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
